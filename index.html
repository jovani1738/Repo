Stranger Things is a Netflix original TV series. The genres include science fiction, supernatural, horror, mystery, and period drama. The show was released on July 15th, 2016. It was written and directed by Matt and Ross Duffer. It received positve reviews and is getting renewed for a second season containing nine episodes in 2017.

The plot follows the disappearance of a young boy named Will, and a telekinetic girl who helps his friends in their search, while the boy's brother, mother and the police chief start their own investigations. 12-year-old Will Byers vanishes mysteriously. Will's frantic mother, Joyce, searches for him while Police Chief Jim Hopper launches his own investigation. Will's friends Dustin, Mike and Lucas discover a telekinetic girl who claims to know Will's location. As they search for answers, they unravel a series of extraordinary mysteries involving secret government experiments and unnerving supernatural forces. The show is set in 1983 in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana.
